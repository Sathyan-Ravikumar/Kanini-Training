FAQ about dog traning

Dog traning is beneficial for your dogs or puppy.It will creates a bond between you and your pet, it can also help you keep your dog safe and provides mental stimulation for pup. These are some of the reasons to train your dog or pup

  • How to House Train a Puppy?

    New puppies have no understanding of the proper place to eliminate until their owners housebreak them. House-training is an important part of caring for a puppy. With some basic knowledge of how to train your puppy, they’ll master it in no time.

  • When to Socialize a Puppy?

    During your puppy’s first three months of life, they will experience a socialization period that will permanently shape their future personality and how they will react to their environment as an adult dog. Gently exposing them to a wide variety of people, places, and situations now makes a huge, permanent difference in their temperament.

  • What are the best traning tips for dogs

    Some useful traning tips are

    • Learn about dog body language
    • Train with high-value treats
    • Catch your dog being good
    • Always be happy when your dog comes to you, whether you called him or not
    • Provide the right amount of exercise and mental stimulation
